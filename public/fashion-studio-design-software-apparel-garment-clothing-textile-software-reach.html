What does REACH Fashion Studio do?

Previous Next

Fashion products are, by definition, short lifecycle products.

Fashion value chain is also among the most complex and geographically exploded.

Retail outlets of fashion products could be in a country, designers in another, manufacturers in another and many sub contractors and suppliers in various other countries.

Designing garments according to changing consumer tastes and trends, getting these manufactured and delivered to retail outlets or to consumers directly is a complex task. That there are very many collections in a year, and that each collection has many garments, and the inability to deliver on time will lead to stock outs and mark downs makes this process a very complex, time sensitive one.

REACH Fashion Studio shortens product development cycle times by enabling design teams to use AI enabled easy to use tools to create designs easily and fast, and enhances business velocity by enabling collaboration between design, marketing and production teams.

If you an organization in the fashion, garment, clothing, apparel, textile or related sectors, REACH Fashion Studio will save you money,time and hassle.

Government

Vast majority of apparel manufacturing firms are Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises( MSME s).REACH Fashion Studio will save product development costs and enhance their competitiveness.

Globally, apparel/fashion is a USD 2.4 trillion market. According to Mckinsey Consulting :" If it were ranked alongside individual countries’ GDP,the global fashion industry would represent the world’s seventh largest economy."

General CAD products do not meet fashion/apparel industry user requirements well. Fashion/apparel CAD needs many industry specific functions.

Adoption of intelligent digital tools like REACH Fashion Studio among apparel MSMEs have been very low for reasons of price, awareness, availability of local talent, local support etc.

Sewing machine is an engineering marvel. Also, a social engineering tool. Sewing machines brought millions of women the ability to participate in economic activity, even when they could not migrate to towns or family circumstances did not allow them to work outside for 8 hours, and consequent empowerment. Intelligent Digital tools like REACH Fashion Studio do the same today.

Today, a woman in a remote village can design textile and garment, create patterns for it and manufacture the garment herself or collaborate with a manufacturing unit to manufacture her designs.



She can also offer fashion design services as an outsourced service to a larger firm located elsewhere in the country or outside.

Apparel industry is among the most fragmented industries. Quite a lot of technology enablement has happened on the demand side ( eg. online sales catalogues, AI to understand consumer preferences and make recommendations etc.).However,on the supply side, tech enabling millions of apparel MSME s is work far from done.

If you are a policy maker or a government representative, you can contribute to making apparel firms in your region competitive by creating awareness of and helping implement REACH Fashion Studio. We offer awareness sessions, workshops, pilot projects, training, e-learning content, support for market and finance linkages and linkages to industry experts.
